Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Pulse is an analytics tool that helps website owners understand how visitors use their sites. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have.

Who we are

Pulse is an indie analytics product. If you have any questions about this policy, email us at nopeman9989@gmail.com.

What we collect

When you use Pulse on your own website (as a Pulse customer), we collect:

  • Your email address and password (for your Pulse account)
  • Your website URL and project name

When Pulse is installed on a website you visit (as a visitor of a site using Pulse), the tracking script may collect:

  • The URL of the page you visited
  • Which buttons or links you clicked (text and element type only)
  • Which form fields you focused on (field name only, never the content)
  • Your browser's user agent (browser type and version)
  • The referring URL (where you came from)
  • Timestamps of events

We do not collect:

  • Your IP address
  • Any form input values (passwords, emails, messages, etc.)
  • Cookies or persistent identifiers
  • Personal names or contact details of site visitors

How we use your data

Data collected by the tracking script is used solely to provide analytics to the website owner who installed Pulse. We do not:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Use your data for advertising
  • Share your data with anyone outside of Pulse
  • Build profiles across multiple websites

Data storage

Event data is stored securely on our servers. Account data (email and password) is managed by Supabase, which handles authentication. Passwords are never stored in plain text.

Data retention

Event data is retained for as long as you have an active Pulse account. If you delete your account, your data will be deleted within 30 days.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Ask questions about how your data is used

To exercise any of these rights, email nopeman9989@gmail.com.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We'll update the date at the top of this page when we do. Continued use of Pulse after changes means you accept the updated policy.
